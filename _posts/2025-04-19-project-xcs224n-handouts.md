XCS224N Handouts collects the lecture notes and suggested readings for XCS224N, Stanford's professional version of CS224N, Natural Language Processing with Deep Learning. This is my fork of the handouts repository, kept in my account as a study reference.

## The concept

CS224N/XCS224N is one of the canonical courses for learning how modern NLP works, from word vectors and dependency parsing through recurrent networks, attention, and transformers. The handouts here are the supporting reading material rather than code, so this repo is essentially a curated bookshelf I keep alongside the course.

There's no build or runtime to speak of; it's reference material. I keep it forked so the notes stay pinned in my account for offline study.
