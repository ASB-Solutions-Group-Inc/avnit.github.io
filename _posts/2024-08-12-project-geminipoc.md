Infrastructure-as-code for a proof of concept built around Google's Gemini models on Google Cloud. The repo is written in HCL, so the substance is Terraform: standing up the GCP resources needed to exercise Gemini in a controlled project.

The README is just an ASCII-art banner (a POC repo through and through), so I'll keep this honest: this was a quick 2024 experiment from my day job orbit — I work with customers on GCP AI and security architecture, and small disposable Terraform POCs like this are how I validate an idea before it goes anywhere near a real design. It stays public as a timestamp of that experiment.
